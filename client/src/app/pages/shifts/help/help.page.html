Welcome To Shift Tracker!

Adding a Shift

To Add a Shift, click on on the toolbar. A shift has the following required properties: Start and End Time, base Rate, Employer, Location and payment Status.

It can also have up to three different Penalty Rates. Click on Add Penalty to include a different penalty rate in a different time within a shift.

To add and delete a location or employer, click on Manage Location or Manage Employer. You need to add at least one employer and one location before adding a shift.

Careful! When deleting a location or employer, you'll delete all shifts associated with it.

Editing and Deleting a Shift

To edit or delete a shift, click on the shift and drag it to your left. This will present the buttons for both options.

Filters

You can visualise your shifts using filters. Our current filter options are By Employer, By Location, By Date, By Date and Categoryand By Status . Click on Show All Shifts to display all records.

Logging Out

You'll remain logged in if you close a tab or your browser. To log out, click on on the toolbar.
